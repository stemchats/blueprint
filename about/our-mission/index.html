Our Mission

Blueprint is an initiative under STEMchats, a student-led, non-profit organization that aims to impact underrepresented students and inspire them to create further impact in their own communities

The Blueprint program works to uplift high school researchers and their stories.

Blueprint is composed of two main parts: Blueprint professional youth scientific journal that features student researchers and their work, as well as Magnify, a blog that explores independent researchers and their motivations behind their projects.

What Is Blueprint?

Blueprint is a professional scientific journal for high school students. We aim to feature independent research and guide students through the scientific review and publication process, regardless of access to high-quality labs or other resources.
